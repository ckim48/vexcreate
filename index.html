About

What is the VEX Korea Open?

The VEX Korea Open is a national robotics event in Korea that brings together VEX competition programs under one tournament brand, giving students a shared, high-quality robotics experience.

For 2026, the VEX Korea Open will run the VEX IQ Robotics Competition (VIQRC), designed for elementary and middle school students using the VEX IQ platform. Teams build robots with plastic, snap-together parts and learn core skills in problem-solving, coding, and teamwork.

Through these divisions, the VEX Korea Open aims to provide an authentic engineering environment where students at different levels can take on the same season’s game in age-appropriate ways while sharing one connected event experience.

About Us

CREATE

CREATE is a robotics and STEM education partner that works with schools, coaches, and student teams to bring competition-focused learning into the classroom.

The organization supports teams with training resources, mentoring, and event planning so that students can design, build, and program robots that perform under real match pressure.

CREATE helps connect schools, coaches, and organizers to build a sustainable robotics ecosystem in Korea.

Hosted by

LEX

LEX Consulting is an academic training and STEM program organization in South Korea that specializes in robotics education, competition hosting, and global STEM partnerships.

The company works with leading international schools and educational institutions to deliver high-quality academic programs, Olympiad-level coaching, and robotics competitions that prepare students for global challenges such as the VEX Robotics Competition.

As the official hosting partner of 2026 VEX Korea Open, LEX Consulting ensures a professional, well-organized, and student-centered competition experience supported by experienced mentors and event specialists.
