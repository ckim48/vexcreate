About

VEX CREATE KOREA

VEX CREATE KOREA gives students in Korea the chance to experience a high-level robotics tournament with global standards in production, game design, and team experience.

The competition is designed for elementary, middle, and high school teams who want to go beyond classroom projects and take on an official VEX. Teams design, build, and program robots that must work reliably under real match pressure.

With support from the REC Foundation and CREATE, VEX CREATE KOREA aims to grow a strong robotics ecosystem in Korea by connecting schools, coaches, and students through meaningful STEM events.

Hosted by

LEX

LEX Consulting is an academic training and STEM program organization in South Korea that specializes in robotics education, competition hosting, and global STEM partnerships.

The company works with leading international schools and educational institutions to deliver high-quality academic programs, Olympiad-level coaching, and robotics competitions that prepare students for global challenges such as the VEX Robotics Competition.

As the official hosting partner of VEX CREATE KOREA, LEX Consulting ensures a professional, well-organized, and student-centered competition experience supported by experienced mentors and event specialists.
