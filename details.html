Event Overview

General Info & Schedule

Incheon · Yonsei University International Campus V5RC · VIQRC · CREATE Programs Elementary · Middle · High School

The 2025–2026 CREATE U.S. Open Robotics Championship C.R.C. Asia-Pacific Championship – South Korea is a regional qualifying event for the 2026 CREATE U.S. OPEN in the United States. The event invites teams from China (including Hong Kong and Macau), South Korea, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Australia to compete at Yonsei University International Campus in Songdo.

Alliance champions and Excellence Award winners across VEX and CREATE events (excluding CREATE X and CREATE DRONE) will earn advancement to the 2026 CREATE U.S. OPEN. Hundreds of outstanding teams from across the Asia-Pacific region are expected to participate.

Within this championship, a VEX Korea Open Skills Challenge is offered as a dedicated sub-event for Korean teams, providing a focused platform for driver and programming skills under the same event umbrella.

Key Participation Rules

  • Teams must be officially registered for their respective VEX or CREATE programs for the 2025–2026 season.
  • Grade Level Eligibility: Elementary, Middle, and High School divisions, depending on the program.
  • Engineering Notebooks are strongly recommended and may be required for certain judged awards (e.g., Excellence, Design).
  • All robots must pass inspection before participating in Qualification Matches or Skills Challenges.
  • Registration is limited to 100 team slots and follows a first-come, first-served policy.
